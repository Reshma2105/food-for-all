About Us

Food Donate is a passionate initiative started by a group of socially conscious individuals from Vizag, committed to addressing the dual challenges of food waste and hunger in our community.

Our journey began in 2020 when we witnessed the heartbreaking contrast between mountains of wasted food at events and restaurants, and the empty plates of those struggling to find their next meal. We knew there had to be a better way.

Today, we've built a network connecting food donors with organizations serving the hungry. Our technology platform makes it easy for anyone with surplus food to donate, and for organizations to receive the nourishment they desperately need.

We believe that access to food is a fundamental human right, and that reducing food waste is one of the most impactful ways we can care for both people and our planet.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem that prevents food waste while ensuring that no one in our community goes to bed hungry. We aim to bridge the gap between surplus and need through technology, compassion, and community engagement.

Every day, we work towards our vision of a world where food is valued, not wasted, and where everyone has access to nutritious meals.
